# Twinkle Twinkle Little Star

## Origin
Based on the French melody "Ah! vous dirai-je, Maman" (1761). English lyrics by Jane Taylor (1806). Mozart composed 12 variations (K.265) on this melody in 1781. One of the most recognized melodies worldwide.

## Musical Details
- **Key:** C major
- **Time Signature:** 4/4
- **Tempo:** ~100 BPM
- **Range:** C4–A4 (major 6th)
- **Difficulty:** Beginner

## Analysis
Same melody as "Baa Baa Black Sheep" and the "Alphabet Song." The AABA form makes it easy to memorize. Stepwise motion (C-D-E-F-G-A) with the signature leap of a 5th (C→G) at the start.
